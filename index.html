The Arvada West Choral Music program has established itself as having one the premiere programs in the United States with an invitation to perform at the 2017 National ACDA Conference in Minneapolis. Numerous choirs have also been selected to perform at CMEA conferences in the past several years (the top state honor for high school ensembles) – including 7 appearances since 2009. The choirs also consistently receive straight “Superior” ratings at festivals and competitions.
AWHS choirs have performed in numerous cities and prestigious venues across the United States, including Los Angeles, New York City (Carnegie Hall), Washington, D.C. (Kennedy Center) and Chicago (Orchestra Hall) - and have travelled internationally, performing High Mass at the Basilicas of St. Peter's Rome and St. Mark's Venice.
